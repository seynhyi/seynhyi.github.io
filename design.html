Art of the Web: Final Portfolio



Interfacing Cultures: Final Project

Having heard complaints of friends regarding lack of awareness of short-term mental health resources on campus, as well as lack of information during lengthy wait-times after applying for a counselor through Wellness at Penn, the University of Pennsylvania's offical mental health resource organization, I aimed at redesigning a platform that attempted to make the application process more efficient.
